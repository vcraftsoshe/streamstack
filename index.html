How StreamStack Works

Simple. No Surprises.

You don't lose access to anything. You don't change how you watch. We sit between you and the platforms, handling billing so you don't have to.

01
🎯

Pick Your Platforms

Tell us which platforms you want: Netflix, Neon, Disney+, Prime Video, Apple TV+. Mix and match whatever your household actually watches.

02
💳

We Handle the Billing

We pay each platform on your behalf and collect one consolidated payment from you. One bill. One date. No surprises in your bank statement.

03
📺

You Watch as Normal

Log into Netflix, Neon, Disney+ same as you always have. Nothing changes about the apps or how you use them. Just one less headache.
